About PAF-IAST

Pakistan's Premier Applied Sciences University

The Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is a collaborative venture with select Austrian and Chinese Universities, located in Haripur, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

PAF-IAST is at the fulcrum of the rapidly evolving global job market for highly skilled human resources, transforming Pakistan's youth into a national dividend. Every faculty member is trained in Austria and China, bringing world-class expertise to our students.
